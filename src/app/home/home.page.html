Frequently Asked Questions

How do I return a defective or faulty product?

We stand behind all of our Clothes and gear. If your product is defective or faulty and it's been more than 30 days since your purchase, start a return and send your item back.

How do I return a Dankie Jesu store purchase?

Purchases made at Dankie Jesu retail stores must be returned to the store. Please note that our store returns policies may differ from our online returns policies. Make sure that you ask the store if you have questions about their policies.

How do I make an online purchase?

1. Select an item that you desire. 2. Choose your desired colour, size and quantity 3. Click "Add to Cart" Note If you wish to add more items to your cart, do so by individually going to each item and adding it to your cart. 4. When you are satisfied with the items you desire, go to your cart, this is the button with a basket. 5. in your cart will be all the products that you wish to purchase, at the bottom, click "PLACE ORDER". 6. You are now in the payments page, choose your purchase method, between DELIVERY and COLLECTION. 7. You will see payment information that shows the items you wish to purchase and each item's price, you will also be noted of the delivery fee, if you chose the Delivery method, you will incur a delivery fee of R 100 (one hunderd rand only). A total amount due will be calculated and presented. Furthermore you will see your reference number which you will have to use when you transfer the money with the banking details that are also provided on the screen Transfer the money into the bank account provided on the app.

How do I track my order?

1. Go to your profile 2. Click "Pending Orders" to see a list of your order purchases 3. If you have multiple orders, scroll until you see the one you want to track and the click on "Track my order" 4. On this page, you will see a list of items you have in this order, total amount due, the company's banking details, the scheduled delivery date and the order status. 5. Each order status has a small description used to assist you in finding out the progression of the order

How do I cancel an order?

You are entitled to cancel your order prior to the point at which you confirm your order purchase, if you go pass that point and you want to cancel the order, you should not pay the amount required to process your order, a consultant will contact you after a few days of not making the payment to confirm the order cancellation process. Should you wish to return the product thereafter, you can do so in accordance with our Returns Policy.

Place your order

Do a bit of shopping in your app and add the desired items to your shopping cart and then place the order when you are ready to make the purchase and your purchase will be place in pending orders. You will be provided with a reference number to use when you send the transaction to the bank that is provided for you.

Deposit cash amount

If you want to deposit the amount via a bank branch or ATM, use the provided banking details and use the reference number we provided you with as your reference. For mobile and online banking, the banking details are the same for you too. Below are the banking details that you should always deposit to.

Bank Name : Capitec

Track your order on the app

Go to your profile => PENDING ORDERS.
If you have any pending orders, they will all appear here. Choose one of the orders to track their progress by clicking "TRACK MY ORDER"

Here you will see a list of all the items you have placed in this order, after the list will be the order details, at the very bottom, you will see your order status with the following four steps.

1 - Order Received
This means that your order was received and will be processed as soon as a cash deposit has checked out.

2 - Order is being processed
After you have made the cash deposit and it has been validated, the order status will move to this stage. This means that DankieJesu and its affiliates are preparing your order, this typically

3 - Ready for Collection/Delivery
During this phase, your order is either in transit to your residential address, or it is ready for you to come to the store and collect your merchandise.

4 - Delivered/Collected
After you have received your order, the status will change to Delivered or Collected - respectively; this order will then move from your pending orders to your order history.


About Dankie Jesu ®


Dankie Jesu

Dankie Jesu is a Christian clothing brand that caters for all genders, all races, and all ages. It started off as a hashtag started by the founder and designer, Lunga Duma at his church Assemblies Of God Kagiso in 2015.

He then designed his first T-shirt and sold all the merchandise thereafter from his car boot in 2017. The brand grew, expanded and was officially launched in 2018, 16 June at Assemblies Of God Kagiso, and now has opened its doors at 5630 Moilwa Street, Kagiso.

Dankie Jesu meaning Thank you Jesus designs its own merchandise, sports wear, casual and formal clothing for all genders at an affordable price.

Visit @DankieJesu on Instagram and Facebook for more info

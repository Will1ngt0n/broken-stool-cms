Frequently Asked Questions

How do I return a defective or faulty product?

We stand behind all of our Clothes and gear. If your product is defective or faulty and it's been more than 30 days since your purchase, start a return and send your item back.

How do I return a Dankie Jesu store purchase?

Purchases made at Dankie Jesu retail stores must be returned to the store. Please note that our store returns policies may differ from our online returns policies. Make sure that you ask the store if you have questions about their policies.

How do I make an online purchase?

1. Select an item that you desire. 2. Choose your desired colour, size and quantity 3. Click "Add to Cart" Note If you wish to add more items to your cart, do so by individually going to each item and adding it to your cart. 4. You are now in the payments page, choose your purchase method, between DELIVERY and COLLECTION. 7. On this page, you will see a list of items you have in this order, total amount due, the company's banking details, the scheduled delivery date and the order status. 5. How do I cancel an order?

You are entitled to cancel your order prior to the point at which you confirm your order purchase, if you go pass that point and you want to cancel the order, you should not pay the amount required to process your order, a consultant will contact you after a few days of not making the payment to confirm the order cancellation process. Should you wish to return the product thereafter, you can do so in accordance with our Returns Policy.

About Dankie Jesu ®


Dankie Jesu

Dankie Jesu is a Christian clothing brand that caters for all genders, all races, and all ages. It started off as a hashtag started by the founder and designer, Lunga Duma at his church Assemblies Of God Kagiso in 2015.

He then designed his first T-shirt and sold all the merchandise thereafter from his car boot in 2017. The brand grew, expanded and was officially launched in 2018, 16 June at Assemblies Of God Kagiso, and now has opened its doors at 5630 Moilwa Street, Kagiso.

Dankie Jesu meaning Thank you Jesus designs its own merchandise, sports wear, casual and formal clothing for all genders at an affordable price.

Visit @DankieJesu on Instagram and Facebook for more info
